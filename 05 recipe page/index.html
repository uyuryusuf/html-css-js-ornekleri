Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. T his classic omeleffe combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

    Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

    Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

    Instructions

  • Beat the eggs: : In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed. You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffıer texture.
  • Heat the pan : Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  • Cook the omelette: : Once the butter is melted and bubbling, pour in the eggs. Tilt the pan to ensure eggs evenly coat the surface.
  • Add fillings (optional): When the eggs begin to set at the edges but are stili slightly runny İn the middle, sprinkle your chosen fillings over one half of fhe omeleffe.
  • Fold and serve: As the omelefte continues to cook, carefully lift one edge and fold it over the fillings. Let it cook for another minute, then slide it onto a plate.
  • Enjoy: Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed.
